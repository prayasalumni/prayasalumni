Benefits of becoming the Life Member of the Prayas Alumni Association

  • National Alumni Network: National Alumni Network: Join a nationalwide network of prayas alumni,connecting you to a diverse and influential community around the nation.
  • Institute-Alumni Bridge: The Alumni Association serves as a vital link between the prayas Institute students and its graduates, fostering collaboration and communication.
  • Stay Informed: Receive the newsletter and other prayas alumni publications to stay updated on the latest news and events at your alma mater.
  • Local Chapters: Access Alumni Chapters by participating in regular chapter get-togethers and exclusive events.
  • Special Institute Events: Be invited to special events and ceremonies hosted by the Prayas Institute and Alumni Chapters nationwide.
  • Batch Reunions: Attend milestone batch/class reunions like the 10th, 12th, 13th, 15th, 17th, and 50th, with assistance from the Alumni Association in organizing other batch reunions upon request. Reunion photos and videos are made available through the website here after the events.
  • Digital Networking: Connect with alumni via meeting, Yahoo groups (batch/class-wise), Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, and more.
  • Academic Services: Obtain your academic transcripts and other certificates conveniently through the Alumni Association.
  • Mentorship: Benefit from mentoring opportunities provided by senior alumni, including participation in various academic programs.
  • Souvenir Shop: Explore the Alumni Association's own souvenir shop, where you can find memorabilia and merchandise celebrating your alma mater.
  • Exclusive Email ID: Receive a permanent email ID (e.g., xxxxyop@ prayasalumni.org) by sending a request to alumni_office@ prayas.ac.in, providing your personal details, alumni ID, and more.
  • Credit Card Facility: Enjoy access to an exclusive credit card facility designed for alumni, offering competitive terms, customized designs, and rewards programs. Insurance Policy: Secure your financial future with a comprehensive insurance policy tailored to the needs of our alumni.
  • Campus Tours: The Alumni Association can assist in organizing campus tours for you and your family, allowing you to revisit and explore the campus.
  • Campus Facilities: Enjoy discounted rates for alumni in the Visitors Hostel, book VH rooms at prayas, and access other campus facilities. Additionally, you can visit hostels, the library, departments, labs, and even meet with faculty members with the Alumni Association's help.
  • Privacy and Security: Rest assured that your life membership database is maintained securely by the Alumni Association and not shared with unauthorized parties. It also offers Degree Verification Services for pre- and post-employment screening companies.
